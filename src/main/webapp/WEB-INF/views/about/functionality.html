Spring Library Application

People Books About

Functionality:

  • pages for adding, changing and deleting a person
  • pages for adding, editing and deleting a book
  • page with a list of all people (people are clickable - when clicked, go to person's page)
  • page with a list of all books (books are clickable - when clicked, go to book page)
  • a person's page showing the values of his fields and a list of the books he has taken
  • page of a book showing the values of the margins of that book and the person's name, who took this book
  • on the book page, if the book is taken by a person, a button appears next to his name "Release book"
  • on the book page, if the book is free, a drop-down list appears with all people and the "Assign Book" button
  • all input fields are validated using @Valid and Spring Validator
